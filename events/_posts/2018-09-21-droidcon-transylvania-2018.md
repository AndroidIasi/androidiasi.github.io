## First droidcon Transylvania

droidcon is the place to meet the global Android community, listen to expert speakers, find out about all the latest Android advances and connect with peers.

droidcon brings together technology and the people that do amazing things with it. It’s the professional conference that presents the best of what Android has to offer.

Some of the open topics during this edition: Data model/ APIs, Cloud Services, Design, UI & UX, Architecture, Testing, Open source libraries, best practices, Android Things, Artificial Intelligence and other topics.

Take a look at [the sessions list](http://www.droidcon.ro/program/sessions/talks/) and decide if you want to join.
